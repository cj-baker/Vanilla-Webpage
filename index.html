About Adriene Mishler

Adriene Mishler is a professional Yoga instructor and the Producer/Host of Yoga with Adriene, a free YouTube channel focused on teaching everyone mindfulness and how to, as Adriene says, "find what feels good". Adriene has become an inspiration to many individuals, including myself, to do something that is easier said than done: Show up for yourself. Her videos promote self-love, positivity, and a physically healthy lifestyle. Adriene's audience is anyone wanting to try or continue practicing yoga, she leaves space on her channel for beginners, intermediates, and pros alike. If the Yoga with Adriene YouTube channel is not content for you, she also now has an SVOD (Streaming Video On Demand) Service that you can find on her FWFG (Fidn What Feels Good) Website.
